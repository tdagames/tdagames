About the Game

Polyomino Blocks is a strategic puzzle game! Drag and drop polyomino shapes onto a 9x9 grid. Clear rows, columns, or 3x3 blocks to earn points. Plan ahead carefully - the game ends when you can't place any more shapes!

Rules

Three random polyomino shapes appear at the top. Drag & drop them onto the 9x9 grid. Points depend on shape size. Bonus points are awarded when a row, column, or 3x3 block is cleared. Game ends when no moves remain.

Enjoy the game!
