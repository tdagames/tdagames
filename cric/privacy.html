Privacy Policy - Live Cricket TV & Score

Effective Date: November 20, 2025

Drydev (“we”, “us”, or “our”) created the Live Cricket TV & Score app as a free service. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used and protected.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect personal data directly. However, we integrate third-party services that may collect anonymous, non-personal data to improve the app’s performance, serve ads, or send notifications.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following services:

  • AdMob (Google): For showing ads. May collect device ID, IP, app usage. (Google Ad Policy)
  • Firebase (Google):
    • Analytics – Tracks anonymized usage data
    • Crashlytics – Captures crash reports
    • Remote Config – Allows content updates
    (Firebase Privacy)
  • OneSignal: For push notifications. Collects device info, language, and engagement. (OneSignal Privacy Policy)
  • Facebook Audience Network: Serves ads and tracks engagement. (Facebook Privacy)
  • Qureka: May display game content or ads under its own privacy terms.

3. Permissions Used

  • INTERNET – To load ads, content, and sync data
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – To detect if the device is online
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – For sending alerts and offers
  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID – To access the advertising ID for ad personalization

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or directly share your personal information. Third-party SDKs may process anonymized data under their own privacy policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover a child’s data has been collected, we will promptly delete it.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical measures to secure your data. However, no internet method is completely secure.

7. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted here. Continued use of the app after updates means you accept the new terms.
